About Lomo Trace

Lomo Trace is an offline-first private travel photo log application that allows you to capture, organize, and cherish your travel memories without compromising your privacy.

Feature Overview

  • Offline access: Use the app anywhere, with or without an internet connection.
  • Privacy-focused: Your photos and data are securely stored and not shared with third parties.
  • User-friendly interface: Easily navigate through your travel memories and themes.
