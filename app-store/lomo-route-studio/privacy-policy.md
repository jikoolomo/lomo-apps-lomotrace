# LOMO Route Studio Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-12

LOMO Route Studio is a local-first iPhone app that turns selected photos into short route-story videos.

## What the app accesses

- Selected photos from your photo library
- Metadata contained in those selected photos, including timestamp and GPS location when available
- Generated video files created on your device

## How the app uses that data

- To sort selected photos by time
- To group photos into places or moments
- To generate route previews and route-story videos
- To save exported videos back to your photo library when you choose to save them

## What the app does not do

- No account creation
- No cloud upload
- No backend storage
- No social posting on your behalf
- No sale of personal data

## Data retention

LOMO Route Studio is designed for local processing on your device. Selected photos and generated outputs are handled locally unless you choose to save the exported result to your photo library.

## Third-party software

The app uses third-party software components required for photo access, media playback, and Flutter runtime support. Those components are used to operate the app locally on your device.
